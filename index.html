Solana Runts Club is a collection of 5000 uniquely generated, eclectic punk style NFTs on the Solana blockchain. Ownership of a Solana Runt secures a voting membership in the club.

Club Mission

1. Support the Solana Foundation through Stake Pools and/or Delegation Program.

2. Provide new membership opportunities by developing special edition Solana Runt art.

3. Donate 10% to charity.

Mint a Solana Runt to join the club.

1 SOL per NFT.

Countdown to Mint

A club wallet has been set up to receive 5% of the sales/royalties, and initially all $SOL will be put into long-term staking. Members will be able to influence how the club wallet is spent to achieve its mission via a future voting system.

The club wallet will also own 20 Solana Runts for distribution in community events and marketing purposes.

What is the total Solana Runts Club supply?
The total supply of Solana Runts is 5000.

When is the mint date?
Minting will begin on Nov 4 2021 8am EST.

What is the minting cost?
Each Solana Runt will cost 1 SOL to mint.

What Solana wallets can I use?
You will be able to use four of Solana’s main wallets Phantom, Sollet, Solflare and Ledger.

Where can I buy or sell my Solana Runts?
After minting, you can list on FTX.US, Digital Eyes, SolSea, Magic Eden and Solanart.

Do I get more voting power in the club if I own more than one Solana Runt?
Yes, each Solana Runt carries one vote.

Who ultimately owns and controls the club wallet?
The Solana Runts Club team own and manage the club wallet.

What is this special edition art you talk about?
We intend to release special edition dynamic/animated SVGs and digitally framed and signed versions. The club members will be able to vote on the quantity released and how this is managed.

What is the Solana Runt ethos?
Solana Runts Club is all about using the power of the collective to do good.
